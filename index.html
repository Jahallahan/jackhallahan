Hi, I’m Jack. I’m an experienced UX designer, originally from Brisbane, Australia and now living in London. My background is in visual communication, and I have around 5 years of experience in digital design.

I have worked for product startups and agencies. Currently, I’m part of the growing product design team at Geckoboard.

Day-to-day, I work across the entire product design lifecycle, from user research to interaction design and visual design. I’m passionate about solving the most important and challenging problems.

Prototyping is a key part of my design process. I use a range of tools including code to produce low- and high-fidelity prototypes to test with users and iterate.
